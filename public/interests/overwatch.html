overwatch

i started playing overwatch in november of 2019, right during the dr ziegler event. i've been hooked ever since! my approximate peaks on each role are as follows:


  • tank : 3000sr, unplaced in ow2
  • dps : masters 4
  • support : grandmaster 5

my favorite heroes to play on each role are as follows:


  • tank : d.va, orisa, zarya, sigma
  • dps : widowmaker, ashe, cassidy, sombra
  • support : ana, illari, mercy, kiriko, baptiste

i hope to update this page frequently, documenting my adventures! yes i fit like every stereotype of a female overwatch player. that's fine. i don't aim to be subversive in any way. i just have way too much love for both widowmaker and illari than i know what to do with. i also mostly play quick play, since i find ranked more stressful than it's worth. but i'm absolutely happy with my peaks on my most played roles, especially granted that i don't play actively seeking to improve. that's just my skill level, and i'm a-okay with that!
